Delete Your Elys Account

Option 1: Delete in the app

1. Open the Elys app
2. Go to Settings
3. Tap your profile at the top
4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
5. Confirm the deletion

Option 2: Request via email

Send a deletion request to support@elys.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

What data is deleted

  • Your account and profile information
  • All products and expiry dates you have entered
  • Your notification settings

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Subscription billing is managed by Google Play or the App Store — cancellation must be done separately through your store account.
