Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

The Elys app is developed by Infosoft. This page informs users about our data handling practices.

Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address, display name, profile photo (via Google Sign-In or email registration)
  • App data: products and expiry dates entered by the user
  • Subscription data: subscription status and type
  • Diagnostic data: crash reports and app usage statistics

How We Use Your Data

  • Managing your account and syncing your data across devices
  • Sending expiry reminder notifications
  • Managing your subscription
  • Fixing bugs and improving the app

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase – authentication, data storage, analytics, crash reporting (privacy policy)
  • Google Sign-In – optional Google account login (privacy policy)
  • RevenueCat – subscription management (privacy policy)

Camera and Photos

The app requests camera and photo library access solely for setting a profile photo. Images are stored securely in Google Firebase Storage.

Data Retention and Deletion

When you delete your account, all personal data and app data are permanently deleted. Account deletion is available in the app under Settings → Edit Profile.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.
